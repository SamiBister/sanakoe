# Sanakoe User Guide

A kid-friendly vocabulary quiz app to help you practice for language tests! 🚀

---

## Getting Started

### Opening the App

1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Safari)
2. Go to the app URL (e.g., `http://localhost:3000`)
3. You'll see the welcome screen in Finnish by default

### Changing Language

Click the language button in the top-right corner to switch between:

- 🇫🇮 **Suomi** (Finnish)
- 🇬🇧 **English**

---

## Adding Your Words

You have two ways to add vocabulary words:

### Option 1: Upload a CSV File 📁

Perfect if your teacher gave you a word list file!

1. Click **"Lataa CSV"** (Upload CSV)
2. Click **"Selaa tiedostoja"** (Browse files) or drag-and-drop your file
3. The app accepts `.csv` and `.txt` files
4. You'll see a preview of your words
5. Check the word count at the bottom

**CSV File Format:**

```
koira,dog
kissa,cat
talo,house
```

Both comma (`,`) and semicolon (`;`) separators work automatically!

### Option 2: Type Words Manually ✏️

Perfect for creating your own practice list!

1. Click **"Syötä Käsin"** (Enter Manually)
2. Type words in the table:
   - **Left column (Prompt):** The word you'll see as a question
   - **Right column (Answer):** The correct answer
3. Press **Tab** to move to the next cell
4. New rows appear automatically when you fill the last row
5. The counter shows how many complete word pairs you have

**Tips:**

- Both columns must have text for a word pair to count
- Your entries are saved automatically - come back anytime!
- Click **"Clear All"** to start fresh (asks for confirmation)

---

## Taking the Quiz

### Starting

1. After adding words, click the big **"🚀 Aloita Koe"** (Start Quiz) button
2. The quiz begins immediately with a timer!

### Answering Questions

1. You'll see a word at the top of the card
2. Type your answer in the input box
3. Press **Enter** or click **"Tarkista"** (Check) to submit

### Correct Answers ✅

- You'll see an encouraging message like "Hienoa!" (Great!)
- The next word appears automatically after 1 second
- A star shows you got it right!

### Wrong Answers → Practice Mode 📝

If you get a word wrong:

1. You'll see the correct answer highlighted in yellow
2. **Practice Mode** begins - type the correct answer 3 times
3. A counter shows your progress (1/3, 2/3, 3/3)
4. After 3 correct repetitions, you return to the quiz
5. The word will appear again later for another try

**Note:** Practice mode helps you learn - it doesn't count against your score!

## Understanding the Progress Bar

At the top of the quiz screen, you'll see:

| Item           | Meaning                           |
| -------------- | --------------------------------- |
| **3 / 10**     | Words completed / Total words     |
| **5 yritystä** | Number of answer attempts (tries) |
| **02:34**      | Time elapsed (MM:SS)              |

---

## Viewing Your Word List 📋

Click the **"≡ Sanat"** (Words) button in the top-right corner anytime to see all your words!

### Status Icons

| Icon | Meaning                                                |
| ---- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| ✅   | **Resolved** - You answered correctly                  |
| 🔁   | **Unresolved** - Not answered yet                      |
| ⚠️   | **Mistake** - Wrong on first try (will practice later) |

### Filters

- **Kaikki** (All) - Show all words
- **Ratkaisemattomat** (Unresolved) - Words you haven't answered yet
- **Virheet** (Mistakes) - Words you got wrong

The current word is highlighted with a yellow border.

---

## Results Screen 🏆

After answering all words, you'll see your results:

### Your Score

- **Total Words:** How many words you practiced
- **Total Tries:** How many attempts you made
- **Total Time:** How long the quiz took

### Words to Review

A list of words you didn't get right on the first try - great for extra practice!

### Personal Records 🎉

The app remembers your best scores for each word list!

- **New Record!** appears when you beat your previous best
- Records are saved for both **tries** and **time**
- Confetti celebrates your achievement!

### What's Next?

- **"🔄 Pelaa uudelleen"** (Play Again) - Same words, new random order
- **"🏠 Uusi lista"** (New List) - Go back to add different words

---

## Tips for Success 💡

1. **Take your time** - Accuracy matters more than speed
2. **Use Practice Mode** - It really helps you remember!
3. **Check the Word List** - Review words you're unsure about
4. **Beat your records** - Try to use fewer tries each time
5. **Practice regularly** - Short daily sessions work best

## Troubleshooting

### "My words didn't save"

- Words are saved in your browser's local storage
- They persist across sessions on the same device/browser
- Clearing browser data will erase saved words

### "The CSV won't upload"

- Make sure it's a `.csv` or `.txt` file
- Check that each line has exactly 2 columns (word, translation)
- Try using comma or semicolon as separator

### "The quiz won't start"

- Make sure you have at least 1 complete word pair
- Both prompt and answer must be filled in

### "I see an error screen"

- Click **"Try Again"** to retry
- Click **"Go to Home"** to start fresh
- If problems persist, try refreshing the page

## Privacy & Data

- 🔒 **All your data stays on your device**
- No accounts or sign-ups required
- Nothing is sent to any server
- Your word lists and records are stored locally in your browser
